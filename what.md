# Climate Change by Lex

## What is climate change
It is the long-term change of temperature and weather patterns worldwide

## Causes
- Burning fossil fuels
  - ICE cars
  - Factories
  - Power plants
- Farming livestock
- Volcanic Activity
- Cutting down forests

## Results
- Glaciers melts
  - Rising sea levels
    - Flooding
- Heat waves
- Drought
- Warming oceans
- Animals harmed
- More common extreme weather
